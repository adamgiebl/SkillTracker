A Walk in the Park

Download and unpack starter_files

This exercise is the very first in the Walk in the Park track, and the only thing you have to do, is to download the zip file: walk_in_the_park.zip and unpack it to the folder on your own machine, where you will keep the library of tiny exercises and examples.

The first three exercises are already included - and for future exercises, you'll simply have to add more files into the same folder.

Enjoy, and don't worry - it's going to be a walk in the park ...
